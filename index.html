About Dr. Keshav Agarwal

Dr. Keshav Agarwal is a distinguished urologist with exceptional academic achievements and specialized training in Robotics and Minimally Invasive Urology. As a dual gold medalist from AIIMS, New Delhi, he brings excellence in both clinical care and surgical expertise.

Currently serving as a Consultant in the Department of Urology at Yashoda Medicity, Ghaziabad, Dr. Agarwal has completed advanced fellowship training in Robotics and Minimally Invasive Urology from Max Superspeciality Hospital, New Delhi. His expertise spans complex urological procedures, kidney transplantation, and cutting-edge robotic surgery techniques.

With a commitment to academic excellence and patient care, Dr. Agarwal actively contributes to urological research and has presented his work at prestigious international conferences including the American Urological Association, European Association of Urology, and Urological Association of Asia.
